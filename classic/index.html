About Us

As Vietnam emerges fully onto the world economic stage, new and constantly-evolving demands are placed on business executives in all industries. To successfully manage these challenges, today’s executive must possess strong analytical skills, the ability to think strategically, and effective leadership capabilities. To meet these challenges head on, the University of Hawaii, Shidler College of Business has delivering its first-rate Executive MBA program that will train, guide, and prepare Vietnam’s top executives who want to elevate their management skills. We are receiving applications for Fall 2020 Intake for both Hanoi and HCMC Campuses.

The Program Advantages

Highly Ranked - The University of Hawaii, Shidler College of Business is recognized for its excellence in international management and ranked among the top 20 best colleges in the US for international business by U.S. News & World Report.

AACSB-Accredited – This is the only AACSB-accredited MBA program in Vietnam which assures a high-quality, relevant, and internationally-recognized MBA.

Top Faculty - All classes are taught by world class faculty with hand-on experience from the University of Hawaii and selected universities in the US. Our faculty use a variety of interactive teaching formats and methods designed to engage and challenge experienced business leaders.

Flexible Schedule - Executive MBA classes are conveniently scheduled in the evening and on weekends to allow busy professionals to attend class and continue working full-time

Asia-Pacific Focus - A pioneer among U.S. business schools in international business, the University of Hawaii distinguishes itself with its focus on the Asia-Pacific Region.

Rigorous Curriculum - Students are challenged to solve complex business problems in a global environment, apply strong analytical and strategic thinking to multifaceted business issues, and to develop sophisticated management skills.

Extensive Alumni Network - There are nearly 30,000 University of Hawaii Shidler College of Business Alumni living in 40 countries around the world. The Vietnam Executive MBA program has been in operation in Hanoi since 2001, in Ho Chi Minh City since 2007 and has developed a senior alumni network across Vietnam and many other countries.

Summer Study in Hawaii – Students may like to take up to two elective courses during the Summer terms in Hawaii.

Tuition Subsidy *- Thanks to our donor, Mr. Jay Shidler, the tuition fee of The Shidler Executive MBA program in Vietnam is substantially subsidized, $23,040 compared to $52,896 for the same program in Hawaii. Tuition and fees are subject to change without prior notice.

Electives

Rigorous and challenging, the Shidler College of Business Executive MBA curriculum is designed to sharpen tools, refine skills, and put global challenges in perspective. You will experience professional growth, and possibly even rekindle your own entrepreneurial spirit.

The Shidler Executive MBA has a well-thought modern curriculum which carefully integrates qualitative and quantitative aspects of modern management in a results-oriented structure. We understand that leadership is not only about analytical skills, but also about managing human relationships in the workplace. The core curriculum covers accounting, finance, marketing, organizational behavior, as well as explores new technology, entrepreneurship, e-business, and global business strategy with an Asia-Pacific focus.

Entry requirement

A bachelor degree Minimum 5 years of work experience TOEFL iBT from 61 or IELTS from 5.5 (if English was not the medium of instruction at undergraduate studies. Admitted students with TOEFL below 100 or IELTS below 7.0 are required to take 2 advanced English courses offered by the University of Hawaii, in parallel with the Executive MBA program)

Tuition and Fees*

The tuition fee for the Shidler Executive MBA program in Vietnam is US$480 per credit (or US$23,040 for the program with 48 credits). This fee does not include costs for textbooks, copyrighted materials, English Language Institute (ELI) classes, and optional summer study at the University of Hawaii at Manoa in Honolulu, Hawaii, USA. Tuition can be paid with small instalments for each course.

BUS 696 (3 Credits)

In the last 2 semesters, instead of writing a thesis, students are required to conduct a substantial team-based strategic project for a client organization, under faculty supervision and guidance. This practical study integrates all the material and knowledge learned from previous courses and applies to the real workplace.
